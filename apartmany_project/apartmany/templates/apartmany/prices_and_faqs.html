Price Information

Our prices per person per night are in the range of 18 to 25 Euros. The price depends on the period (season/off season) and the number of people in a studio or an apartment. We will be happy to send you the complete pricelist via email. A single adult in a studio or an apartment has to pay the equivalent to two adults. Additionally to accomodation costs, a local tax fee for adults and children older than 12 years of 1 Euro per person and night has to be charged.

Discounts

  • Children up to 12 years:
    50 % off full price (in studios only)*
  • Children up to 12 years on a sofa-bed:
    60% off full price (in studios only)*
  • Adult on a sofa-bed:
    20% off full price (in studios only)

* Discounts for children are only valid if there are 2 adults paying the full price in the same studio. Two children accommodated by themselves in 1 studio are paying full/adult prices.

Frequently Asked Questions

Below, you can find important information regarding check-in and check-out, reservations, facilities, etc. Click on the headers to collapse the topics and then click on the questions to see the answers. If your question however remains unanswered, please do not hesitate to contact us via e-mail or call.

When can I check-in and check-out?

You can check-in after 2pm on the day of your arrival. The heck-out time is at 10am the latest.

Do you offer early/late check-in (after 8pm) ?

We recommend you to come after 2 pm, however it might be possible that your room is available earlier. Please contact us beforehand if you need to check-in earlier than 2pm. On the other hand, if you think you will arrive to our place later than 8pm, it's not a problem, just please let us know.

Do you offer late check-out ?

Unfortuntaly, during the season we do not offer late check-outs. It might be available off-season, upon request.

How can I make a reservation enquiry?

We arrange all reservations through emails. If you have any questions, you can contact us telephonically or message us on facebook, however reservation must be done via email. Please send us an email to vysny.m@stonline.sk with number of people and the time period you are interested in and we will send you a price list and a list of available apartments/studios suitable for your needs. Alternatively, you can simply fill out this form.

When is the reservation valid?

Your reservation is confirmed once we received your deposit. The deposit is usually 50% of the price.

How and when do I pay?

We usually require a deposit to be transfered to our account, prior to your arrival. We kindly ask you to pay the rest on the day of your arrival. We only accept cash payments in the local currency (Euro).

What happens if need to cancel my reservation?

If, for any reason, you need to cancel your vacation at our place and you already paid the deposit, we will be happy to move your vacation to any agreed future term.

Can I get my deposit back when I cancel?

In case that we are still able to rebook your room to other guests, we will be happy to return the deposit to you, minus any fees for bank transfers.

How do I find you if I am coming by car?

You can find us very easily. When you get down from the highway, you follow the road signs to JASNA. Then you just follow the road all the way to Demänovská Dolina. We are located at the very beginning of the valley, on the right side of the road, just next to a big hotel called Tri Studničky.

How do I reach you if I am coming from Liptovský Mikuláš Main Station?

From the Liptovský Mikuláš station (train and bus station are on the same location), you can simply take a short bus drive to the stop Demänovská Dolina Autocamping. From the bus stop, follow the road for around 500m (5 minutes) and you will see us on the right side of the road, just next to the big hotel called Tri Studničky.

Do you offer car parking?

Absolutely. You will have no troubles to fit your car on our spacious private parking lot. The parking lot is locked down during the night so you can sleep peacefully!

How far is the ski-center from the apartments and how can I get there?

We have a prime location above the parking P5 Pavcina Lehota where, in busy periods, visitors of Jasna ski center are asked to park their car and use the shuttle bus for the rest of their trip. You, as our guest can use your own car to get all to way to the ski lifts. The ride takes only around 8 minutes. You can also use the free shuttle bus that stops pretty much on our doorstep.

Can a baby bed be placed in the room?

Yes, we have a baby cot, baby bathtub and baby chair available for you, free of charge. Please let us know prior to your arrival that you will need these.

How are the apartments/studios equipped?

You can check the details of each apartment and studio in the section Apartments, Studios. Generally however, every studio has a fully equiped kitchen with a stove, microwave, fridge and all necessary utensils. There are towels and hairdryers in the bathroom and a TV and a radio in the bedroom.

Are there any toiletries in the bathroom?

Unfortuntaly, we do not provide toiletries :(

Are the rooms heated/air-conditioned?

During winter, the apartments are well heated so you can rest comfortably after skiing, in a warm cozy place. We do not have air-conditioning, however it is rarely too hot during summer since we are surrounded by trees.

Where can we store our skies and ski boots?

We have a dedicated ski room with enough space for all our clients. We even have ski boot dryers so you can rest assured your boots will be dry and warm the next morning when you are about to hit the slopes!

Can we rent bikes?

Yes! We have 8 mountain bikes (including small bikes for children) available for our guests free of charge.

Do we have to pay extra to use the sauna?

Use of our sauna is charged with a small fee of 5 euros per person. But you know what is cool? Cooling down is taken care of the river flowing right through our property!
