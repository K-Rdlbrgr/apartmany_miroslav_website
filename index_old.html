What to do in Demanovska Valley

The Demanovska Valley has a lot of differnet activities to offer which are located just a few minutes away from our accomodation. The valley provides perfect skiing and hiking conditions as well as being home for several aquaparks, thermal spas, historical sites, museums and Slovakia's longest cave system.

HIKING

Low Tatras offer a large number of trails for hiking and trekking with different levels of difficulty, from the demanding, which requires a certain degree of preparation to those that are designed for families with children or beginners.

SKIING

Located just next to the ski resorts of Jasna and Ziarce, you'll be able to minimize travelling time and enjoy your skiing on Slovakia's longest ski slopes even more. We also provide a storage room for all your equipment.

THERMAL

All within 20km, you can find the aquaparks Tatralandia and Besenova, thermal swimming pools in Liptovsky Jan and the recreation centre of Liptovsky Mara which together offer a variety of watersports activities and relaxing opportunities.
